The task

In the following game, you are asked to repeatedly choose between two alternatives. Some of the choices will be rewarded with a smiley face (☺), and your goal is to collect as many smiley faces as possible.

In addition to the participation fee ($0.40), you will receive an additional bonus of $0.01 for every smiley face you collect.

After completing the task, you will receive the Amazon Mechanical Turk payment code.

You may stop participating at any time by closing the browser window. By pressing the "Start" button below you indicate that you agree to participate in this game.

Please press the "Start" button below when you are ready to start.

Consent for Participation in a Research Study

Principal Investigator: Prof. Yonatand Loewenstein


DESCRIPTION: We are researchers at the Hebrew University of Jerusalem doing a research study about decision making. In this task, you will make repeated choices between two alternatives and be rewarded, according to your choices in some of the trials. To be included in Describe research procedures here, including any screening procedures that are used to determine eligibility. You will not be asked any personal questions. Participation should take about 5 minutes.

RISKS and BENFITS: The risks to your participation in this online study are those associated with basic computer tasks, including boredom, fatigue, mild stress, or breach of confidentiality. The only benefit to you is the learning experience from participating in a research study. The benefit to society is the contribution to scientific knowledge.

COMPENSATION: As a compensation to your participation in this online task, you will be granted 40 cents. Additional bonuses may apply as defined by the task instructions.

PLEASE NOTE: This study contains a number of checks to make sure that participants are finishing the tasks honestly and completely. As long as you read the instructions and complete the tasks, your HIT will be approved. If you fail these checks, your HIT will be rejected.

CONFIDENTIALITY: Your Mechanical Turk Worker ID will be used to distribute payment to you but will not be stored with the research data we collect from you. Please be aware that your MTurk Worker ID can potentially be linked to information about you on your Amazon public profile page, depending on the settings you have for your Amazon profile. We will not be accessing any personally identifying information about you that you may have put on your Amazon public profile page.

Any reports and presentations about the findings from this study will not include your name or any other information that could identify you. We may share the data we collect in this study with other researchers doing future studies – if we share your data, we will not include information that could identify you.

SUBJECT’S RIGHTS: Your participation is voluntary. You may stop participating at any time by closing the browser window or the program to withdraw from the study. Partial data will not be analyzed.

For additional questions about this research, you may contact: Ohad.Dan@mail.huji.ac.il
